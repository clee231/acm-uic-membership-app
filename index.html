We have a slack! What's that? It's a online group of ACM's chat rooms (Similar to IRC or Group messages). People can usually answer your ACM related questions. Users on ACM's Slack frequently use it for casual/semi-professional discussion about cool CS factoids, breaking news, SIG and group information, general discussion, and conversing with fellow ACM friends. If you have your UIC email you can sign up at uicacm.slack.com (Don't worry you'll get this information again at the end)

You can sign up for National membership or just UIC membership. UIC memberships last till the end of that school year and cost $10. A receipt will be issued to you once you pay the membership dues. Please KEEP your receipt; this is the proof you have paid your dues for the school year and is to complete this form. National memberships are 1 year from the date of signing up. The ACM@UIC honors national memberships at no additional charge to national members. You can sign up for an ACM national student membership for $19.00 at https://campus2.acm.org/public/qj/quickjoin/qj_control.cfm?promo=PWEBTOP&form_type=Student . You will receive an email with a member ID which you should provide below.
